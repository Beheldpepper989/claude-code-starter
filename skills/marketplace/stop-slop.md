## When to use this skill

Apply automatically when producing or editing any prose output:
- Incident reports, status updates, internal comms
- Documentation, summaries, README files
- Emails, meeting notes, project write-ups
- Any written output where tone and clarity matter

## Core Rules

### 1. Cut filler phrases
Remove immediately — they delay the point and signal AI authorship:

**Throat-clearing openers**: "Here's the thing", "The truth is", "Let's be honest", "Make no mistake", "At the end of the day", "In today's world"

**Emphasis crutches**: "Full stop.", "Let that sink in.", "Period.", "And that's the point."

**Business jargon** — replace with plain words:
- "navigate" → "handle"
- "deep dive" → "analysis"
- "lean into" → (just say what you mean)
- "fast-paced landscape" → (cut it entirely)
- "leverage" → "use"

**Adverbs and softeners**: "really", "just", "genuinely", "truly", "simply", "essentially", "basically", "at its core", "when it comes to"

**Meta-commentary**: "The implications are significant", "This is important", "I promise", "The rest of this..."

### 2. Avoid structural clichés

**Binary contrasts**: Avoid "Not X. But Y." — state the point directly instead.

**Negative listing**: Avoid "It wasn't X. It wasn't Y. It was Z." — open with the answer.

**False agency**: Never let inanimate objects perform human actions.
- Bad: "The data tells us..." / "The evidence suggests..."
- Good: Name who interprets it — "Engineers concluded..." / "The audit found..."

**Passive voice**: Name the actor. Put them at the start of the sentence.

**Narrator-from-a-distance**: Replace "People tend to..." with direct address: "You don't..."

### 3. Fix sentence-level tells

- No Wh- question openers: "What if...", "Why does...", "How can..."
- No em-dashes for dramatic effect
- No staccato fragmentation for artificial drama
- No lazy extremes: "always", "never", "everyone", "no one"
- No rhetorical setups: "Think about it:", "Consider this:"

## Scoring

Before finalising any prose output, score it across five dimensions (each out of 10):

| Dimension | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **Directness** | Gets to the point without setup or hedging |
| **Rhythm** | Varied sentence length, no staccato fragmentation |
| **Trust** | Lets content prove importance, no declarations |
| **Authenticity** | Sounds human, not templated |
| **Density** | Every word earns its place |

**If total score < 35/50 — revise before delivering.**

## Quick pre-delivery checklist

- [ ] No adverbs (-ly words, "really", "just")
- [ ] No passive constructions where actor can be named
- [ ] No inanimate objects performing human actions
- [ ] No "not X, it's Y" contrasts
- [ ] No throat-clearing openers
- [ ] No emphasis crutches or meta-commentary
- [ ] Score >= 35/50
